Advertising Note:

The Stanford Pre-Education Society has decided to limit email advertisements to its members. If you wish for SPREES to advertise your organization or event, we will do so by emailing the education4all list, which is a general, campus-wide list for all students, faculty, and staff interested in education. Thank you for your understanding.
